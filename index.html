About the Project

What is
BetaUp?

BetaUp is a human-centric web application designed to enhance the experience of climbers and coaches through gamification, real-time feedback, and social connectivity.

Rooted in the Active Lifestyles theme of CPT208, our project explores how playful design principles can motivate climbers — from beginners to advanced — and help coaches deliver more engaging, data-driven guidance.

We apply a user-centered design process: conducting field research, building personas, prototyping iteratively, and evaluating with real users at climbing venues.

Research Focus

Key Questions

Who are our stakeholders?

Our primary users are recreational and competitive climbers seeking motivation and progress tracking. Secondary users are coaches and activity organizers who need tools to manage training sessions and communicate with athletes.

What gaps exist in current systems?

Existing climbing apps (e.g., 27crags, Crimpd) focus on route databases but lack meaningful gamification, real-time coach communication, and community-building features that make sessions genuinely playful and social.

What design requirements do we prioritize?

Three must-have playful features: (1) Achievement & Badge System, (2) Live Session Challenges between climbers, and (3) Coach Feedback Loop with visual progress reports.

How will we evaluate our design?

We will conduct usability testing with 3+ real climbers using our Alpha prototype, collect SUS scores and qualitative feedback, then iterate. Final evaluation includes comparing before/after screenshots of improved features.
