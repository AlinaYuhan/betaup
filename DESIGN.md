# BetaUp — Design System

## Color Strategy: Committed
Orange (#e8500a) carries 30-40% of visual presence. Dark near-black base. Warm charcoal surfaces.

## Colors (current, to be improved)
- Background base: #080a0c (near-black, warm-tinted)
- Surface: charcoal-brown direction, not blue-gray
- Accent: #e8500a (burnt orange, --summit)
- Text primary: #fff7ee (warm white, --chalk)
- Text body: #d8d1c6
- Text muted: #9f988d
- Moss green: #6da36f (secondary accent, used sparingly)

## Typography
- Display: Bebas Neue — large, impactful, climbing poster energy
- Body: DM Sans — clean, readable, modern
- Mono/labels: Space Mono — technical, precise

## Surfaces
- Cards: warm dark charcoal, subtle warm borders
- Glass: used rarely, purposefully
- No blue-gray (#222831 is wrong — too cold for a climbing brand)

## Motion
- Fade-up on scroll reveal
- No bounce, no elastic
- Ease-out-quart

## Photo Treatment
- Hero: climbing gym photo, overlay at 0.45-0.55 opacity (let the image breathe)
- Images should feel like action shots, not stock

## Anti-patterns
- No gradient text
- No side-stripe card borders
- No identical card grids
- No blue-gray surfaces
- No SaaS-cream / corporate feel
